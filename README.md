# 🎵 HexaPlayer - Simple Local Music Experience

[![Download HexaPlayer](https://img.shields.io/badge/Download-HexaPlayer-green)](https://github.com/Axel354/HexaPlayer)

## About HexaPlayer

HexaPlayer is an Android app designed to play music stored directly on your device. It works without ads and is completely free. This player focuses on local music files, giving you a smooth and clean way to listen to your favorite songs. The app has a dark theme, an equalizer for sound adjustments, and uses modern design principles to keep things easy on the eyes and simple to use.

## Topics and Features

- Plays music from files saved on your device
- No ads for an uninterrupted experience
- Dark mode to protect your eyes at night
- Equalizer controls to customize your sound
- Built with modern tools and libraries like ExoPlayer and Kotlin
- Compatible with Android devices
- Free and open-source for transparency and trust
- Uses a clear material design interface

## 🛠 Installing HexaPlayer on Your Android Device

Follow these instructions to install the app after downloading the APK file:

1. Open the **Files** or **Downloads** app on your Android device.
2. Find the HexaPlayer APK file you just downloaded.
3. Tap the APK file to start installation.
4. If this is the first time installing an app outside of the Google Play Store, your device will ask you to allow installations from unknown sources. Follow the prompts to enable this setting.
5. Once allowed, the installation process will continue automatically.
6. When installation finishes, tap **Open** to launch HexaPlayer.

## 🎶 Using HexaPlayer

Once installed, using HexaPlayer is simple.

### Play music from local files:

1. Open the HexaPlayer app.
2. It will scan your device’s storage for music files automatically.
3. Browse your music library by artist, album, or song.
4. Tap a song to play it.
5. Use the built-in equalizer to adjust sound quality to your preference.
6. Switch to dark mode anytime in the app settings for a comfortable viewing experience in low light.

### Supported Music Formats

- MP3
- WAV
- AAC
- FLAC (if supported by your device)
- OGG

## ❓ Troubleshooting

If you run into issues, try these steps:

- Make sure your Android version is 5.0 or higher.
- Check that the APK file downloaded completely and is not corrupted.
- Enable permissions for storage access on your device.
- Restart your device after installation.
- Use the manual library scan if some songs do not show up.
- Clear app cache if the app slows down or crashes.

If problems persist, you can find help in the GitHub repository’s issues section.
